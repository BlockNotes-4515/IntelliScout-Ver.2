About the Project📽️ :

Our IntelliScout Ver.2 is an advanced monitoring robotic car (SpyBot) designed for real-time surveillance and reconnaissance. Equipped with AI-powered object detection, remote control capabilities, and autonomous navigation, it efficiently monitors environments with live video streaming and obstacle avoidance.
